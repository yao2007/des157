Online Reading Auxiliary

Dyslexia is a specific learning disability in reading. According to the definition given by Wikipedia, dyslexia, also known as reading disorder, is characterized by trouble with reading despite normal intelligence. Problems may include difficulties in spelling words, reading quickly, writing words, "sounding out" words in the head, pronouncing words when reading aloud and understanding what one reads. It affects 3–7% of the population, however, up to 20% may have some degree of symptoms. It has several different diagnosis, which are Central dyslexias, Peripheral dyslexias, and Neglect dyslexia.

The cost of e-reading is much lower for people who have dyslexia instead of buying dyslexia fridendly book, because it is quick and easy to adjust the way text are displayed and fonts typed through browser extension. For example, people with dyslexia often reversals the alphabets, at this time a text font with heavy bottom and light top like OpenDyslexic will help them recognize letters correctly.
